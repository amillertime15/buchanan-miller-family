Weekend Events

Three days of family, faith, and fun in Katy, Texas

Friday, July 21

🎉 Meet & Greet

Kick off the weekend with a backyard BBQ cookout in the Elyson community. Food trucks, yard games, splash pads, dominoes tournament, and a StoryCorps recording station to capture our elders' stories.

⏰ 6:00 PM – 9:00 PM  |  👕 Casual / T-Shirts
Saturday, July 22

🔴⚪ Banquet & Program

The main event! Formal dinner with our family program: history presentation, talent showcase, memorial tribute, Family Feud tournament, DJ and dancing. Dress code: RED & WHITE.

⏰ 5:00 PM – 10:00 PM  |  👔 Formal Red & White
Sunday, July 23

🙏 Worship & Farewell

Family worship service at a local church followed by a farewell brunch. Group photo, distribution of family history materials, and see-you-next-time hugs.

⏰ 11:00 AM  |  👗 Sunday Best

Travel Info

✈️ Flights

Fly into George Bush Intercontinental (IAH) or William P. Hobby (HOU). Best prices: book March-May 2028. Set Google Flights alerts for Houston.

🏨 Hotels
