🌳 Digital Family History Project

Interactive Family Tree

FamilySearch.org (Free) — Build a collaborative tree, archived at Library of Congress. MyHeritage — Photo colorization & animation. The-FamilyTree.com — Visual interactive tree with photos and stories.

Oral History Recording

StoryCorps App (Free) — Record guided interviews with elders at the Friday event. Stories archived at the Library of Congress. Set up a "recording booth" with good lighting and prepare prompt questions.

Digital Cookbook

Collect recipes via Google Form. Design with Canva or Blurb. Include photos and stories behind each dish. Add QR codes linking to video tutorials.

Family Website

Central hub for tree, photos, recordings, cookbook, events. Options: Google Sites (free), Notion (free), Squarespace ($15/mo).

Photo Digitization

Google PhotoScan app to scan old photos. Shared Google Photos album for everyone to contribute. Consider digitizing old VHS tapes.

Heritage & DNA

Research IGBO Tribe Nigeria connection. Consider African Ancestry DNA testing for culturally-specific lineage tracing.
